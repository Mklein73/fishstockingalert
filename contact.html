Get in Touch

The best way to reach us is by email. We read everything and try to reply within a day or two.

Email
contact@fishstockingalert.com

For general questions, feedback, and data requests. Also use this address for privacy or data deletion requests.

We especially welcome:

  • Bug reports — if something looks wrong, broken, or missing, let us know
  • State data suggestions — which state should we add next?
  • Water requests — if a specific water is missing from the data, tell us
  • Feature feedback — what would make the site more useful for your fishing?

This is an independent project, so responses may take a day or two during busy periods. We appreciate your patience and every message we receive.
