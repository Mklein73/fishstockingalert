Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Fish Stocking Alert is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What We Collect

  • Email address: only when you sign up for stocking alerts. We do not collect your email for any other purpose.
  • Basic analytics: we use standard web analytics to understand how visitors use the site (pages viewed, general traffic patterns). This data is aggregated and not tied to any individual.
  • Dark mode preference: stored locally in your browser via localStorage. This never leaves your device.

How We Use It

Your email address, if provided, is used solely to send you fish stocking alerts for the waters you select. We will never use it for marketing unrelated to fish stocking, and we will never sell, rent, or share it with third parties.

Analytics data is used to understand which features are useful and to improve the site. It is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Cookies

This site uses cookies and similar browser storage for the following purposes:

  • Dark mode preference: stored in localStorage in your browser. This never leaves your device.
  • Session management: used to maintain basic site functionality.
  • Third-party cookies: services used on this site, including Google AdSense, Supabase, and Cloudflare, may set their own cookies. These are governed by each service's own privacy policy.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect how some features of the site function.

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services. Each service handles data as described below and is governed by its own privacy policy.

  • Cloudflare: provides hosting, DNS, email routing, and CDN services. Cloudflare may collect IP addresses and traffic data for security and performance purposes.
  • Supabase: provides the database for email alert subscriptions. If you sign up for alerts, your email address and selected water preferences are stored in Supabase.
  • Resend: provides email delivery services. Resend processes email addresses in order to send stocking alert notifications and confirmation emails.
  • Google Analytics: this site uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google may set cookies for this purpose.
  • Google AdSense: provides advertising on this site. Google AdSense may collect browsing data and use cookies for ad personalization as described in the Advertising and Cookies section below.

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third party for their own marketing purposes. The stocking data displayed on this site is sourced from public government datasets (CDFW and other state wildlife agencies) and contains no personal information.

Advertising and Cookies

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this site and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on a user's visits to this site and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website. You can opt out of third-party vendor cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

This site does not knowingly serve personalized ads to users in regions where additional consent is required, such as the European Economic Area, without appropriate consent mechanisms in place.

Data Retention

Alert email addresses will be retained only as long as you remain subscribed. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any alert email, which will permanently delete your address from our list.

Contact & Data Deletion

To request deletion of any data we hold about you, or if you have any privacy questions, contact us at contact@fishstockingalert.com. We will respond within 5 business days.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the site after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
